Battleship

An online, two players, guessing game

How to play Battleship

Battleship is a war-themed board game for two players in which the opponents try to guess the location of the other's various ships. In our version of the game you have 10 seconds to place the boats in the positions of your liking. Then, one of the two players gets to guess first. If he guesses correctlyand hit one of the other player's boats, the box that was clicked will turn red, indicating a hit. If he misses, the box will turn red and the other player can make a guess. This will continue until one of the players destroyed all of the opponent's boats.
